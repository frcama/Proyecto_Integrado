Recomended Cultural Events In Valencia

Date: From May 16 to May 26, 2024

7th Valencia Culinary Festival

There are experiences that happen only once in a lifetime and in a unique setting. The Valencia Culinary Festival returns to the capital of Turia, the city with the best quality of life and food in the world. A welcoming place where chefs, waiters, and producers come together to delight your senses. Explore the international flavor of the Mediterranean at an event for the most hedonistic palates. If you want to transform your curiosity into pleasure, challenge your senses, and share your passion for good taste, this movement is for you.


Workshop Price: €7.50

Date: May 16 at 18:00 CEST

XIII Festival 10 Sentidos: Bauhaus Games. Taiat Dansa

The new production by the company Taiat Dansa dives into play and avant-garde with the help of the children's audience. Boys and girls will enter the theater and embark on an adventure-like stage that will reveal the mysteries of the number 3 and its meanings. An exploration of basic shapes – sphere, cube, and pyramid – and the colors yellow, pink, and black.


Free Workshop

Date: May 16 at 18:30 CEST

In Other Words: Dialogues with Édouard Louis and Roy Galan

It's been ten years since the publication of Édouard Louis' autobiographical book, Goodbye to Eddy Bellegueule, a story that recounts a childhood of homophobia, machismo, racism, alcoholism, and above all, poverty that transcends the walls of his home and makes the narrative universal and political. Following this success, Louis has continued to explore the wounds of his childhood and the reality of the working class with lucidity and empathy. Édouard Louis will discuss his work and the themes within it with writer Roy Galan, who has also clearly depicted how masculinities operate.


Workshop Price: €6

Date: May 16 at 19:00 CEST

Érase

Érase is an unlikely tale of daughters to mothers, of ducks to grandmothers, of milkmaids to hunters. A tale that fell behind the wardrobe and never came out of the wolf's guts again. A story about us, about how no story ever invented has been able to explain how we should care, dance, work, laugh, or kiss.


Workshop Price: €7 - €14

Date: May 16 at 11:00 CEST

XIX PolinizaDos

The 19th Edition of the PolinizaDos Urban Art Encounter returns to the Vera Campus of the Polytechnic University of Valencia from May 13 to 17, 2024. This year, the Encounter will focus on collective processes, relational dimension, and the revision of common discourses in urban art. With the prominent participation of Mexico as the guest country, through cooperation with the INBAL National School of Painting, Sculpture, and Engraving "La Esmeralda", the Encounter aims to explore the diverse perspectives and traditions of urban art, thus enriching cultural dialogue and fostering international collaboration. As part of the classic mural interventions in the Campus spaces, a highlighted feature of this year's proposals is the emphasis on collective production and community practices.


Free Workshop
